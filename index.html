The above map shows plotted locations of fortune 500 company headquarters and Starbucks locations in the US.

Starbucks locations that are in a state that don't have a fortune 500 company headquarter won't be plotted.

The size of the yellow circles corresponds to the total revenue of that company. You can zoom in by double tapping or scrolling in. You can use this map to see that the more starbucks in a location the more fortune 500 companies and the higher the revenue.

Using the buttons, you can hide starbucks locations, company locations, or show both.
